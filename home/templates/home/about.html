About

Who We Are

We are a group of music teachers and pianists who, after decades of teaching had a thought: "Just because we're teaching classical music, doesn't mean our teaching itself has to be old-fashioned!" We created Web Piano Academy to embody that idea; to use all of the knowledge & technology that exists today in order to modernize the way we teach the piano.

What We Do

Here at Web Piano Academy, we believe that there is no one correct way of learning the piano. Too many teachers are stuck in the "old-fashioned" way of teaching which, while it has its benefits, fails to take advantage of the wonderful technological resources that we all have at our fingertips. We have so many helpful tools at our disposal to make learning the piano fun, engaging and FAST!

Your Journey With Us

We are so proud of our courses and we know you'll love them too. Each course has extensive video material that you can progress through at whatever pace you wish. At Web Piano Academy, we know that everybody learns in different ways, that's why all of our courses have accompanying digital music sheets, worksheets, games and many other resources to ensure that you have all the tools you need to learn in the way that is the most productive and enjoyable for you.
